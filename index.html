About Me

Hi, I’m Satheesh Bhukya, an IIT Dhanbad graduate with a Master’s degree in Mathematics and Computing. I am passionate about building scalable AI systems that move from research to real-world deployment.

During my 6-month internship at Novus Hi-Tech Robotic Systemz (ADAS Team), I built a real-time Driver Identification System and benchmarked and optimized Depth Estimation models (Depth Anything series). I improved inference performance by converting models to ONNX format for efficient deployment, supporting integration into a Forward Collision Warning (FCW) pipeline.

I have hands-on experience developing end-to-end ML pipelines — from model training and evaluation to optimization and production deployment using Python, PyTorch, FastAPI, vector databases, and ONNX.

My interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Large Language Models (LLMs), Generative AI, and building real-time RAG-based systems.
