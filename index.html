About Me

Hi, I’m Satheesh Bhukya, an IIT Dhanbad graduate with a Master’s degree in Mathematics and Computing. I have hands-on experience building, evaluating, and optimizing machine learning models for real-world and production applications.

I have worked on end-to-end ML pipelines — from data collection and feature engineering to model benchmarking, performance optimization, and deployment. My work spans Computer Vision and Language Models, including Generative AI and RAG-based systems.

During my 6-month internship at Novus Hi-Tech Robotic Systemz (ADAS Team), I developed a real-time Driver Identification System and benchmarked Depth Estimation models (Depth Anything series). I optimized inference performance by converting models to ONNX format, enabling efficient deployment within a Forward Collision Warning (FCW) pipeline.

My interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Large Language Models (LLMs), Generative AI, and building real-time RAG-based systems.
